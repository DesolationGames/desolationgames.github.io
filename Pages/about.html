About

What is Desolation Games? We are a small company who create simple mobile games for Android devices. Founded in early 2016, we have currently released one app and are in the process of developing another.

Our aim is to produce quality apps with no price at all. Yes, that's right, free apps. Our plan is to let people pay for our apps if they decide it is worth your money. This is achieved through toggled ads (Yep, you can disable ads) or directlty donating.

However, for us to create quality games which you would want to play, we need your help. Our growth is dependent on your support and feedback. Whether you have a suggestion or just rate one of our apps, we are greatly thankful.
