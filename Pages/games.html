Scratch

Before I began creating mobile games for Android, I made games on Scratch. Scratch is a great visual programming language for beginners but also more experienced coders. It has a great online community and I am very thankful for Scratch. It sparked my passion for programming.

Below is one of my games I have created on Scratch. Enjoy!

Amazing Wire

Instructions

You are a magical wire trying to score the most. Beat other players and get to the top of the leader board!

  • Click and hold to go up, Release to go down
  • You get a point for going through a pipe
  • Gain points and unlock special costumes
  • Have Fun! :)
