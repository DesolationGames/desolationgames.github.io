Dodge

The Dodge icon

Dodge the oncoming enemies, Evade the Deadly squares and Claim the highscore.

Dodge is a fun and exciting free game which will keep you entertained for hours. Use your finger to guide your player away from oncoming enemies and claim the highscore. Now including 4 unique Game Modes to chose from.

Install and play our first ever Android game that we, Desolation Games, have created. It may not be the best game on the market but we are sure proud of it!

Features:

  • Use the touch screen to move your player or tilt the device in the 'Tilt' game mode
  • Evade the oncoming squares and triangles
  • Enter the shop to spice up your player
  • Get the highest score and unlock all the Achievements
  • Check who is the best at Dodge with Google Play leaderboards
  • Enjoy the simple and smooth graphics

Dodge is created by "Desolation Games" and contains no ads. Yep, its ad free!
